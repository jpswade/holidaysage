Free express transfers included

Like spending more time in your destination and less time travelling? Good news – free express transfers are included as standard for this hotel. That means you’ll travel to your resort with other holidaymakers, but you’re guaranteed to be dropped off first, second or third on arrival to your hotel, and one of the last three pick-ups on your return transfer.
Please note: This service is only available between 1st May and 31st October. If you’re travelling outside these dates, you’ll catch our usual coach transfers.

Certified Sustainable Hotel

What do we mean by ‘sustainable’?

This hotel has been assessed by an independent organisation that’s on ABTA’s (The Travel Association) list of accommodation sustainability certification bodies. Here are just a few example practices this hotel has to follow to be certified as sustainable:

  • Reduce energy, water and waste
  • Source products locally
  • Support local communities
  • Use reusable, returnable and recycled product

We try to review our hotels every three months to make sure this information is up to date. If a hotel has the Certified Sustainable Hotel label, this means that at the time of booking, the hotel has met an official standard.

Holidays by the coast

It’s easy to venture from the town to Torrox Costa. Here you’ll find popular diving schools, restaurants and a pristine promenade that follows the coastline. It’s also worth visiting the market for local produce at bargain prices.

Historic discoveries

At the coast, you’ll also find a centre around the lighthouse dedicated to the Roman ruins and mosaics that have been discovered in the area. While you’re there, you won’t have far to walk to unwind on Torrox Beach.
